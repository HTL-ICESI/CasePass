# Service Response

## Fields

| Field | Required | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| `issue_date` | Conditional | Required for issued CPR claims. |
| `service_date` | Conditional | Required to compute response windows. |
| `service_method` | Conditional | Personal, post, email, electronic filing/service, other. |
| `AOS_due` | Conditional | Acknowledgment of service deadline where applicable. |
| `AOS_status` | Optional | Not due, due, filed, overdue, unknown. |
| `defence_due` | Conditional | Defence deadline where applicable. |
| `defence_status` | Optional | Not due, due, filed, overdue, unknown. |
| `extension_agreed_flag` | Optional | Captures agreed extensions where applicable. |

## Validation Rules

- Do not calculate AOS or defence due dates without verified service data.
- If a sealed order varies the timetable, store the order-derived date and provenance.
- Deadlines generated by AI must remain proposed until human verified.
