# Representation Types

CasePass must replace generic `attorney` language with England and Wales representation roles. The handoff route depends on whether the receiving professional is taking over conduct of the litigation or acting only for a limited task.

## Role Model

| Role | Product meaning |
|---|---|
| Solicitor on record | The solicitor treated as acting for service and court-record purposes. |
| Instructing solicitor | The person or firm sending work to counsel, advocate or another lawyer. |
| Receiving solicitor | A solicitor receiving the matter or a discrete instruction. |
| Instructed counsel / barrister | Advocacy or specialist advice role; not automatically solicitor on record. |
| Advocate-only | Limited hearing advocacy; does not by itself trigger change of solicitor. |
| Internal reassignment | Same firm, different fee earner; normally no CPR Part 42 change. |

## Required Questions

- Who is currently the solicitor on record?
- Is the recipient taking over conduct of the claim?
- Is the recipient acting only as advocate for a hearing?
- Is this an internal reassignment within the same firm?
- Has Form N434 or a digital notice of change been filed and served?
